NIGHTLIFE INFORMATION - SALERNO

## Main Area
Historic center - where all nightlife happens

## Popular Bars (Erasmus favorites)
- **Sant'Andrea**: Historic bar/kebab spot at Piazza Sedile del Campo
- **Cantina do Malandro**: Small alley bar, fun atmosphere, eccentric owner
- **Punto e Virgola**: Classic bar with occasional karaoke
- **Foyer Cafe**: Distinguished bar, main karaoke venue

## Hangout Spots
- **Santa Teresa** (aka Salifornia): Beautiful city beach
  ⚠️ **DO NOT SWIM - WATER IS VERY DIRTY** ⚠️
  Perfect for hanging out only
- **Piazza Sedile del Campo** (Largo Campo): Cozy square with bars/nightlife

## Clubs
**In city:**
- **Grace Club**: Only city club (very small, weekday Erasmus events)

**Outside city** (ESN organizes buses):
- **Maremò**: Beachside (summer)
- **Dolcevita**: Pool parties (summer)  
- **DSSZ**: Warehouse club (winter)
- **MODO**: Classic club
- **Legopi**: Smaller, closest to city
